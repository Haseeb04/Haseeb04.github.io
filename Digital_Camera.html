Digital Camera

Digital Cameras are used to capture images. Nowadays most of mobile phones have cameras installed in them.
Principles of operation of a Digital Camera:
  • 1) Light enters through and is focused by the lens
  • 2) Through a filter generate separatedata values for red, green and blue colour components
  • 3) On to an array of sensors on to record light intensity
  • 4) Each sensor pruduces an electrical signal which rapresents a pixel
  • 5) An (ADC) converts measurements of light intensity into binary data
  • 6) The pixles are recorded as an array
  • 7) Most likely the camera will use a lossy format of compression but could be lossless
  • 8) Binary data is saved to some form of secondary storage (ie SD card)
